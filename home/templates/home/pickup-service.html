Pickup Service
Shuttle service "Soeki"

Since January 2020, the Trainingshub has been located at Klompenmakersweg in Woerden. Our previous location was next to the Woerden station so that people could also use public transport. It appears that our new location is difficult to reach by public transport because you have to book in advance for a spot on the bus and then have to walk a bit. We regret this! So we have found a solution: a Shuttle Service!

Do you want to follow a training with us and do you travel by public transport? Then state that you want to use the shuttle service and you will be picked up and brought back by 'Soeki' the Subaru, from and to the station of Woerden. There is room for 4 people and full is full. It's free of charge, but there is a tip jar on our location.
