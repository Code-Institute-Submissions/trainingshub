About

The Traingshub is a (retraining) training and knowledge center where you can go with all your questions about the use of Dutch Sign Language (NGT) and interpreting for deaf and hard of hearing people. We devise and provide training. Independent but often in collaboration with professionals.

The Traingshub challenges NGT interpreters, writing interpreters and combination interpreters to broaden their skills, take a different interpretation direction or apply an alternative interpretation strategy. We also offer training for the deaf and hearing people.

Our in-service training is divided into four forms. These are the Knowledge evenings, Meet and Greets, training courses and intervision. Everyone with their own objectives and way of doing things. These forms are in turn divided into three levels: The novice interpreter / re-entrant, the interpreter and the experienced interpreter / CODA.

It is up to you as an interpreter to check what you need.


History

In December 2015, we, Suzanne Heuft and Dorothy de Leeuw, started as a company based on the vision that everyone should be part of a (knowledge) network in order to obtain information and good training / education.

Suzanne (right) as a teacher and creative brain, Dorothy as a co-brain, back office employee and RTGS contact person. The team was expanded in 2018 with Kirsten de Ven as hostess and Kirsten provides the tastiest lunches during the training sessions. Together we are committed to interesting, personal and professional post-training for all types of interpreters. Emancipation, improvement of position and fair education for the deaf and a better world for interpreters, deaf and hearing people by bringing everyone together.

The trainers of the Training Hub are thinkers who do. Practitioners who also want to continue to develop themselves. They are enthusiastic, involved and decisive, think in terms of possibilities and stimulate what is going well. With their advice they help you to make your own choices and to achieve your development goals.

The name comes from the American "hub", a junction of connections. Connections are the driving force within networks.

We work with a homely atmosphere in which safety and trust are central. We stand for quality, information, experience, specialism, logical thinking and innovation.
