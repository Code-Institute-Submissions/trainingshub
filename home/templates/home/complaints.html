Complaints procedure

We at the Training Hub do our utmost to make sure that everything goes according to everyone's wishes. Nevertheless, it is possible that you are not entirely satisfied with a course that you have followed. If desired, it is possible to make your complaint known. Below you can read a description of how you can submit your complaint and how it will be handled.

Purpose of the procedure

Registering and handling complaints in a careful, confidential and effective manner, removing the causes and taking adequate measures to prevent a recurrence. In short, promoting the quality of the in-service training and the in-service training organization.

Receipt and registration complaint

You can file your complaint by sending it to the emailaddress: traininghubwoerden@icloud.com. A complaint must be submitted within four weeks of the follow-up training. Complaints that arrive after this period will not be processed. After receiving a complaint it will be processed. The complaint is recorded in a complaint file.

Handling complaint

Within 2 weeks after receiving the complaint, the submitter will receive a confirmation of receipt of the complaint by email. The practitioner investigates the complaint and determines whether it is well founded. Within four weeks of receipt, the complaint must be processed by the Training Hub. If the handling takes a longer period due to circumstances, the submitter will be informed by email. If the solutions offered by the Training Hub are not sufficient for the submitter, the independent in-service training board will respond to the complaint within 4 weeks. All complaints will be treated confidentially.

Measures

If the complaint is found to be unfounded, the complainant will be informed of this by email, with an explanation of the findings. If the complaint is found to be justified, the submitter will be informed by email of the solution or measure to be taken. The solution or measure is implemented within four weeks of the decision being announced. Those involved are informed by the practitioner. In order to promote quality, measures are taken and procedures are adjusted if necessary to prevent a recurrence of the complaint.

Closure

After the complaint has been processed, the complaint registration form is processed and archived at Health Investment. The complaint registration form and accompanying documents are kept for a period of three years.
