Payment

The Training Hub is a progressive company. We are flexible, we like to keep up with the times. That is why we have a modern way of paying: you only pay when you have participated in a knowledge evening or training and you are guaranteed to receive points. On the evening itself or the training itself you can, of course, also pay by pin. You are welcome and can always sign up without obligation, we will keep a place for you and we look forward to seeing you.

We do this because we also know how things are going in life. Your child is suddenly sick, you have a car breakdown, or for some other reason it suddenly doesn't work out to follow that training or knowledge evening. In addition, you are busy people who sometimes already have appointments for next December. Hence our commitment to flexible training (to be followed per day and you receive points per day) and method of payment.

Off course we also have the option to pay in advance via our website. When cancelling you will get your money back or you can use it as credit for another course.
